FAQ

How much coffee is in each sample?

Typically ~18–20g per sample (enough for one guided brew). Some coffees may vary slightly depending on roast and density.

Do I need a grinder?

Not required to start. Whole-bean is recommended, but you can begin with pre-ground too (results may vary).

Where do I start after buying?
