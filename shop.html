FAQ

How much coffee is in each sample?

Typically ~18–20g per sample (enough for one guided brew). Some coffees may vary slightly depending on roast and density.

Do I need a grinder?

Not required for the tasting boxes. We can provide the beans as whole-bean by default (recommended) or help you choose a simple grind solution if needed.

Where do I start after buying?

Go to Start page: start.html and follow the guided steps.
