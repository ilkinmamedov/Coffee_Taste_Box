If it tastes… (quick fixes)

Quick fixes. Change only one thing next time (grind, time, or water temp) so you learn faster.

Sour / sharp

Often under-extracted.

  • Grind a bit finer
  • Use hotter water
  • Increase brew time slightly

Bitter / harsh / dry

Often over-extracted (or too hot / too fine).

  • Grind a bit coarser
  • Use slightly cooler water
  • Shorten brew time a little

Weak / watery

Strength too low.

  • Use more coffee or less water
  • Keep steps the same — change only ratio

Too strong / heavy

Strength too high.

  • Use less coffee or more water
