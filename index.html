Why Coffee Taste Box exists

Less guessing

Most people don’t actually know what they like in coffee — they just buy random bags and hope it’s good. Coffee Taste Box makes it simple: learn your taste once, then buy smarter forever.

What’s usually hard

  • Bag labels are confusing (origins, roast, tasting notes)
  • One “bad” bag can make you think you dislike coffee
  • Equipment/recipes change the taste a lot

What we do instead

  • Same simple recipe → fair comparison
  • Guided taste checklist → clearer preferences
  • Quick quiz → your taste profile + recommendations

You’re not trying to be “correct”. You’re learning what you enjoy.

  • Taste hot → warm → cool
